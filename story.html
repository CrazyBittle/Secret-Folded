The Story of My Girlfriend

My girlfriend, Alyssa, was always unique in ways I couldn’t fully understand. Her fashion choices, especially her black leggings paired with a sanitary napkin, were something she loved. It made her feel secure, comfortable, and, as she would say, "in control." I never questioned her preferences because they were just part of what made her, well, her.

One evening, we decided to settle in for a cozy movie night. The heavy rain outside set the perfect backdrop, and Alyssa, as usual, was snuggled up beside me in her leggings and a cozy oversized sweater. We picked a movie that promised to deliver more than just scares—it was about an otherworldly entity, something beyond human understanding.

The atmosphere in the room shifted as the movie grew more intense. The air felt colder, almost electric. Alyssa squeezed my arm, her body tense. I glanced at her, but something about her expression didn’t seem right. She was staring blankly ahead, her chest rising and falling with rapid breaths.

"Alyssa, are you okay?" I asked, my voice laced with concern.

She nodded, but her response was distant. Her hand clutched her stomach, and her face was flushed. Then, just as the tension in the room reached its peak, something strange happened. Alyssa's body jerked violently as if something unseen was taking hold of her. She gasped, her lips parted, and for a brief moment, it felt as if her body was being inhabited by something beyond human comprehension.

Her eyes rolled back, and the room felt heavier, charged with an unnatural energy. It was as though a force was inside her—moving through her, exploring every part of her, in ways I couldn’t fully grasp. Her body trembled with each passing second, the air vibrating with something dark and mysterious.

"Alyssa?" I said, panic creeping into my voice. "What’s happening? What’s wrong?"

Her gaze locked onto me, but it wasn’t her. The look in her eyes was cold, almost predatory. She opened her mouth, but the voice that came out wasn’t hers—low, guttural, and dripping with an eerie malice. The words were foreign, unintelligible, like something out of a nightmare.

The presence inside her seemed to pulse, and for a brief moment, I thought it was as if it had taken complete control. I didn’t know whether to be terrified or fascinated by what was unfolding in front of me. Alyssa's body seemed to move with a life of its own, a force I couldn’t begin to understand.

Then, just as quickly as it had started, it stopped. Alyssa collapsed back into my arms, her body limp and unresponsive. The eerie silence that followed was almost deafening. I could still feel the remnants of the strange energy that had lingered in the air, but Alyssa was now unconscious, her breathing shallow.

When she awoke, she was disoriented, confused, and clearly shaken. "What happened?" she whispered, her voice trembling.

We spent the rest of the night in silence, holding each other close. Neither of us could make sense of what had just happened. But Alyssa confessed later that she had felt a presence enter her—a foreign force that had moved through her in ways that felt invasive, yet strangely intimate.

We sought help from a spiritual healer, someone who specialized in the paranormal and the unexplained. The healer explained that sometimes entities are drawn to certain energies and that Alyssa's habits might have unintentionally opened a doorway for this force to cross through. The healer performed a cleansing ritual, but the events of that night never truly left us. We were left questioning everything we thought we knew about the world, and wondering if some mysteries were simply beyond our understanding.

Based on True Story.
